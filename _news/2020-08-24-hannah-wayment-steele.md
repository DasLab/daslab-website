<p class="TEXT">
              Improving the stability of RNA vaccines will be important for worldwide vaccine distribution.  We present a biophysical model for predicting RNA degradation in a new preprint led by <b>Hannah Wayment-Steele</b>. Through a variety of mRNA design challenges launched through the <b>OpenVaccine challenge</b> on Eterna by <b>Do Soon Kim</b>, we predict that the stability of any RNA vaccine can be increased at least two-fold either through rational design, as demonstrated by Eterna participants, or algorithmically, through the algorithms RiboTree (developed by <b>Christian Choe</b>) and LinearDesign.
            </p>
