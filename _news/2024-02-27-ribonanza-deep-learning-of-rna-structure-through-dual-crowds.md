<p class="TEXT">
              We've released chemical mapping data on 2.1M  RNA sequences as well as a deep learning model RibonanzaNet, distilled from an 800-team <a href="https://www.kaggle.com/c/stanford-ribonanza-rna-folding/">Kaggle competition</a> and the <a href="https://eternagame.org/challenges/11843006">Eterna OpenKnot challenge</a>. All data and models are publicly available -- check out  our preprint: <b><a href="https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.02.24.581671v1.article-info">Ribonanza: deep learning of RNA structure through dual crowdsourcing</a></b>.
