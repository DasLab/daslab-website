<p class="TEXT">
              We've resolved the <b>SARS-CoV-2 frameshift stimulation element </b> with our hybrid structure determination pipeline Ribosolve, and we report effects of antisense oligonucleotides on this RNA's function and on virus replication in cell culture.  Read more in our <a href="https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.07.18.209270v1">bioRxiv preprint</a>. Many thanks to our colleagues in Glenn, Chiu, d'Souza, and Baric labs and SLAC for this fruitful collaboration,  much of which occurred in the midst of.our university shutdowns.
            </p>
