<p class="TEXT">
              Congratulations to Hannah for successfully defending her Ph.D. in Chemistry!  PhD student <b>Hannah Wayment-Steele</b>  has been awarded  the Chemical Computing Group excellence award from the American Chemical Society! You can read more about the award  <a href="‘https://www.acscomp.org/awards/chemical-computing-group-excellence-award’">here</a>. She will present her work at the ACS national meeting later in 2021. Hannah also  spoke recently on work developing computational methods for mRNA stabilization at Schrodinger's Multiscale Modelling for Biotherapeutics <a href="https://www.schrodinger.com/conferences-meetings/multiscale-modeling-biotherapeutics-symposium-online">symposium</a>.
