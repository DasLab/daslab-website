<p class="TEXT">
              The fourth annual EternaCon is happening on Saturday, August 11, and Sunday, August 12, 2018 at Stanford University. Come join us at Li Ka Shing Center (Room 120) or tune in online. As in prior years, the event is fully organized by Eterna players, and most speakers and panels are by players. We also welcome <a href="http://web.engr.oregonstate.edu/~huanlian/">Liang Huang</a>, <a href="https://biochem.science.oregonstate.edu/content/david-hendrix">Dave Hendrix</a>, and <a href="https://web.stanford.edu/group/riedel-kruse/">Ingmar Riedel-Kruse</a> as scientist guest speakers. More information and late registrations available through <a href="https://eternagame.org/web/news/8835372/">Eterna</a>, and a draft schedule is available <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1nfdGYs1-_yGjc0vWz2OZs3WKnWn5gn0TPanV2ISv_1w/edit#gid=18544493">here</a>.
