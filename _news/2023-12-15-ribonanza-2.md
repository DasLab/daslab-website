<p class="TEXT">
              Rhiju spoke at the MLSB workshop at NeurIPS on Dec. 15, 2023, in New Orleans. We announced results of the <a href="https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/stanford-ribonanza-rna-folding">Ribonanza competition</a>. Impressive models came in from many teams demonstrating the deep learning of RNA structure from a data set involving 2.1M RNA sequences. But it's not over yet -- different models predict different answers for new RNAs --  stay tuned for <b>Ribonanza 2</b>!
