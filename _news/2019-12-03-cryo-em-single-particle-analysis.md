<p class="TEXT">
              In collaboration with Wah Chiu's lab, we're pushing the limits of <b>cryo-EM single-particle analysis</b> to solve structures. An article in <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-019-13494-7">Nature Communications</a> describes our results on a 40 kDa RNA, the SAM-IV riboswitch aptamer. The paper includes analyses confirming that the ligand-binding site can be detected in the RNA.
