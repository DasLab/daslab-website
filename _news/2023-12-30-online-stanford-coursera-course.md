              We've launched an <b>online Stanford Coursera course</b>: <a href="https://www.coursera.org/learn/rna-biology">RNA Biology with Eterna</a>. Learn the basics of RNA biology from the point of view of the molecules' folding and biochemistry. It's a totally free course, targeting students at college level, with interactive components based on the Eterna videogame.
